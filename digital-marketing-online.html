Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers about our Online Digital Marketing Course, SEO training, Google Ads, social media marketing, and career opportunities in digital marketing.
Who can join Online Digital Marketing course?

This Online Digital Marketing course is perfect for beginners, students, entrepreneurs, freelancers, and working professionals who want to build a career in digital marketing or grow their business online.

Do I need any experience for Digital Marketing training?

No prior experience is required. We start from basics like SEO, social media marketing, and website fundamentals and move to advanced strategies like Google Ads and performance marketing.

What will I learn in Digital Marketing course online?

You will learn SEO (Search Engine Optimization), Social Media Marketing (SMM), Google Ads (PPC), Email Marketing, Content Marketing, Affiliate Marketing, and Analytics tools like Google Analytics.

Is Digital Marketing a good career option?

Yes, Digital Marketing is one of the fastest-growing career fields. Every business needs online marketing experts to promote products, increase sales, and grow brand visibility.

Will I work on real projects during training?

Yes, you will work on live projects such as SEO campaigns, social media page growth, Google Ads campaigns, and website marketing strategies for real experience.

Will I get a certificate after completion?

Yes, you will receive a completion certificate in Digital Marketing which can help you in job applications, freelancing, and internships.

Do you provide internship or job assistance?

Yes, we provide internship opportunities, resume building support, interview preparation, and job assistance in Digital Marketing roles.
